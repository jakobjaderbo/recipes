Ingredients:
 - 500 grams of ground beef or pork
 - 1 egg
 - salt
 - pepper

Procudure:
 Knead the ingredients together in a large bowl, do not overdo it. 
 Take a small piece of mince and roll it to a ball and put it on a tray.
 Repeat this procedure until you are out of mince.
 Fry all the meatballs in a pan.
 Serve with browned onions, mashed potatoes and lingonberry jam.
